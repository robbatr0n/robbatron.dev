A tennis roguelite I am building in my spare time. Currently a working prototype
with a basic game loop, rudimentary tennis physics and simple collision detection.
The plan is to expand into a full roguelite structure — per-run upgrade choices,
a tournament bracket progression and increasingly difficult AI. Long-term
goal is a Steam release, though that is still a long way off.
